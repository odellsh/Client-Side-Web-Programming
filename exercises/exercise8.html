Brief Biography

Emma Watson was a junior is now a senior and a Comp Sci major at Appalachian State University. She was born on Febraury 8th, 2000 and has one younger sister. Her first love was swimming and diving, and she was quite successful at it aswell. After starting to swim semi-professionally1, she began to not like the sport as much. It was very mentally draining for a 12 year old, and she was always at either school or the pool. After a tailbone fracture, dislocated shoulder, and hip injury, she finally deciced to retire from the sport and focus on her other preferred hobbies, one of which being music.

Emma started to play the flute in 6th grade, and always seemed to do well with this instrument. Swim had always come first, but after retiring she decided to take music more seriously. She began to practice more outside of school, and her teacher decided to put her in state-wide competitions. At first she would get incredibly nervous on stage and wouldn't perform at her best, but with time she began to love performing her favorite pieces and capturing the audience with her music for just a couple minutes. As much as she loved flute, band, and performing, her high school band teacher retired after her Junior year and the new teacher wasn't as much fun to work with. She decided it was time to quit performing, but she still has her flute and tries to play as often as she can.

After quitting band, Emma was looking for another hobby that she could see herself having a long career in. She mentioned Computer Science to her father, and together they found family friends that would let her job shadow their webpage design projects, and she immediately knew this was something she could seriously see herself doing years down the line.
